THEMS THE RULES, AND BOY ARE THERE MANY



Volleyball, scratch that, sports in general have a ton of rules that uphold the integrity of the game and keep players challenging their limits. Understanding each rule is a task that requires determination and experience, so I have gathered the rules that I believe pertain to a beginner/intermediate player’s understanding of the sport along with some outliers that could help you in many situations of play.

I have read and taken rules out of the “Official Volleyball Rules 2021-2024” approved by the “37th FIVB World Congress 2021.” If you would like to know more about the sport and every single detail it brings, follow the link. For a player just trying to learn the basics, a 15 page article on the proper measurements of a volleyball court seem to be a giant waste of time.
