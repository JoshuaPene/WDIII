PLAYING ACTIONS



  • The ball is in from the moment of the hit of the service (authorized by the 1st referee).
    • The service is the act of putting the ball into play, by the back-right player, placed in the service zone.
    • The first service of the first set, as well as that of the deciding 5th set, is executed by the team determined by the toss.


  • The ball is out of play when a fault occurs.
  • The ball is “out” when:
    • all parts of the ball which contact the floor are completely outside the boundary lines;
    • it touches an object outside the court, the ceiling, or a person out of play;
    • it touches the antennae, ropes, posts, or the net itself outside the sidebands (antennae);
    • it crosses the vertical plane of the net either partially or totally outside the crossing space
    • it crosses completely the lower space under the net


  • Each team must play within its own playing area and space. The ball may, however, be retrieved from beyond its own free zone and over the scoring table in its complete extension.
