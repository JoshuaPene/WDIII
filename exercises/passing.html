BLOCKING



  • Lateral Lunges
    • Hold a weight in your hands.
    • You want to start in a standing position.
    • Lift one leg up and out to form a wide base.
    • Then lean on one side to perform the lunge, then move back up to the starting position and move to the other side.


  • Lunges
    • Start in a standing position .
    • Feet hip-width apart.
    • Step forward so one leg is ahead of your torso and the other is behind. Keep front foot flat.
    • Lower yourself down and maintain a 90 degree angle on both knees.
    • Keep back straight.
    • Push off your front leg and return to your starting position.
