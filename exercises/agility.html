AGILITY



  • Figure 8 Backpedal and Sprint
    • Set up 2 cones on a court about 20-30 feet from each other.
    • You will backpedal in a figure 8 pattern from one cone to the next.
    • Once you reach the cone behind you, you will sprint to the cone now in front of you and repeat the process.


  • Jumping Laterally over Cones
    • Set up a few cones in a line (3-5) for you to jump over.
    • You will jump over the cones and switch sides between each jump.
    • Once you reach the end of the cones, run back to where you started and repeat the process.


  • Forward, Lateral Backpedal
    • Set up 5 cones in a zig-zag pattern across the court spaced out evenly.
    • You will start at one of the cones by sprinting to the first cone.
    • After reaching that cone, you will switch direction and shuffle your feet to the next cone (Keep a solid base and make sure your feet donâ€™t click together when shuffling.)
    • Sprint to the next cone, then shuffle again.
    • Repeat this process for the rest of the zig-zag pattern.


  • Lateral Lunge
    • Hold a weight in your hands.
    • You want to start in a standing position.
    • Lift one leg up and out to form a wide base
    • Then lean on one side to perform the lunge, then move back up to the starting position and move to the other side.
