BLOCKING



  • Deadlifts
    • Feet shoulder-width apart.
    • Grab a bar with weights on some dumbbells with your hands just outside your legs.
    • Lower the bar beneath your knees while keeping a straight back.
    • Lift bar by driving your hips forwards with a straight back.
    • Repeat this motion.


  • Dumbbell Pullover
    • Begin on a weight bench or some kind of elevated surface you can lay on.
    • Extend the weight toward the ceiling, over your chest with your elbows bent.
    • Extend the weights back over your head and hold the weight there for a second.
    • Return your arms to the starting position slowly.
    • Repeat the process.


  • Dumbbell Snatch
    • Hold a dumbbell right over your knee (only one arm at a time).
    • Keep a straight back.
    • Drive your hips forward and move the dumbell up to your chest.
    • Change the positioning of you arm to where you will be pushing the dumbbell upwards instead of pulling it upwards.
    • Push the dumbbell above your head and hold it.
    • Gently lower the dumbbell and catch it at your chest, then slowly lower it back to the starting position.
    • Repeat the process.
